🌍 Welcome to LivingGlobe

What is LivingGlobe?

LivingGlobe is an interactive 3D globe that brings the world to your fingertips. Explore countries, discover cultures, and visualize global data in real-time through beautiful, engaging visualizations.

🎮 How to Use

🖱️
Rotate Globe

Click and drag anywhere on the globe to rotate it around. Explore different regions of the world.

🔍
Zoom In/Out

Use your mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom in for a closer look or zoom out for a wider view.

🎯
Hover for Quick Info

Move your mouse over any red country marker to see a tooltip with basic information.

👆
Click for Details

Click on any country marker to open a detailed information panel with comprehensive data.

🎨
Switch Data Modes

Use the control panel on the left to switch between different data visualizations like Population, GDP, Environment, and Culture.

🌅
Day/Night Mode

Toggle between day and night views to see different perspectives of Earth.

🌟 Features

  • Interactive 3D globe with smooth rotation
  • 40+ countries with comprehensive data
  • Real-time country information and statistics
  • Multiple data visualization modes
  • Beautiful day/night transitions
  • Responsive design for all devices

💡 Tips

  • Country names appear as floating labels around the globe
  • Red markers pulse to indicate interactive countries
  • Use the close (×) button to close information panels
  • The globe continues rotating automatically when not being dragged
